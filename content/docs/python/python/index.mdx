## Introduction

Python is a simple and powerful language, popular for its ease of use and versatility.

It's widely used in data science and AI because it makes working with data and building models easier, with strong community support and many tools for complex tasks.

<Callout>
  Python has become the most used language on
  [GitHub](https://github.blog/news-insights/octoverse/octoverse-2024/#the-most-popular-programming-languages)
  due to the rise of AI, highlighting its importance in the field.
</Callout>
